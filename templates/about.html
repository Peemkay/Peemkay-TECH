About

Engineering software that has to be trusted, not just shipped.

I'm Peemkay, founder and lead engineer at Peemkay TECH — a software practice built around one idea: the sector shouldn't determine the quality bar.

Peemkay TECH began the way most serious engineering practices do — solving a real, high-stakes problem. Early work building secure communications, dispatch management and biometric identity verification systems for a military signal corps set the bar: the software had to work offline, protect sensitive data, and never fail quietly.

That same discipline now carries over into every engagement — whether it's a Django storefront processing real customer payments, a student portal serving an institution, or a new AI-driven idea that needs a technical co-builder, not just a coder for hire.

I work end-to-end: architecture, backend, frontend, mobile and deployment — across Python (Flask/Django) and Flutter, with computer vision and biometric systems as a specialty. Confidential and NDA-based engagements, common in government and defense work, are a normal part of how I operate.
